Design System Approach

Neobrutalist Style

This design system uses a neobrutalist approach with radically different visual styles:

  • Geometric shapes : No border-radius, square and rectangular forms
  • Thick borders : 3-5px, always black for contrast
  • Offset shadows : Black offset shadows (classic brutalist style)
  • Vibrant colors : Contrasted palette (red, turquoise, yellow)
  • Bold typography : Uppercase, tight letter-spacing, high weights
  • Sharp animations : Fast transitions with translation on hover
